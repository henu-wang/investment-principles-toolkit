## About KeepRule

**[KeepRule](https://keeprule.com)** is a free AI-powered investment principles platform designed to help investors build and maintain disciplined, rules-based investing habits.

### Features

- **Investment Principles Library** — Curated rules and wisdom from Buffett, Munger, Graham, and other master investors
- **AI Chat** — Discuss your investment decisions with an AI trained on value investing principles
- **Scenario-Based Decisions** — Practice applying principles to real-world investment dilemmas
- **Bilingual Support** — Full English and Chinese content

### Why Rules-Based Investing?

Research consistently shows that individual investors underperform by 2-4% annually — not from bad analysis, but from emotional decision-making. Rules-based investing addresses the root cause by defining your process before emotions enter the picture.

> *"The most important quality for an investor is temperament, not intellect."* — Warren Buffett
